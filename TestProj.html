About Us

Welcome to the Lucky Shamrock Pub. Discover the charm of a classic Irish pub with modern touches in the heart of Lancaster. We serve traditional Irish meals, premium drinks, and offer a welcoming atmosphere. Coupled with fun events, we seek to provide one of the most engaging experiences within the county, while also serving the community through good ethics and charity.

Our Menu

Here at the Lucky Shamrock Pub, we serve a small, but delectable selection of traditional Irish meals. Come try our iconic Shepherd's Pie, or our tasty Bannocks. Drink a variety of alcoholic beverages, such as sweet Irish Cider, creamy Coole Swan, rough Irish Whiskey, the famous Guinness, and our own homemade Emerald Ale. From Irish stews to local craft beers, there's something for everyone.
